Relations of the roots in polynomial equations

i. Quadratics $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$, with roots $\alpha, \beta$

ii. Cubics $ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d = 0$, with roots $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$

iii. Quartics $ax^4 + bx^3 + cx^2 + dx + e = 0$, with roots $\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \delta$

iv. Quantic with higher order $ax^n + bx^{n-1} + \cdots + k = 0$,

Special cases for cubic equation with roots $\alpha, \beta, \gamma$.
